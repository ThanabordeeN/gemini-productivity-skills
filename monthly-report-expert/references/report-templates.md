# Report Templates

Use these templates to structure your monthly reports.

## 1. McKinsey Style (Strategic / Executive)

**Structure:**

1. **Executive Summary (The Lead):**
   - Main conclusion or recommendation first.
   - Summary of the impact this month.
2. **Key Strategic Pillars (MECE):**
   - Pillar 1: Growth / Efficiency
   - Pillar 2: Risk / Compliance
   - Pillar 3: Capability / Team Development
3. **Data Evidence:**
   - Visual charts (Trend, Comparison, Impact).
4. **Strategic Roadmap:**
   - Recommended decisions for the next 30/60/90 days.

---

## 2. Operational Style (Automation & Process Improvement)

**Structure:**

1. **Summary of Operational Wins:**
   - 1-2 major successes (e.g., "Full roll-out of n8n SalesReport").
2. **Efficiency & Performance Metrics:**
   - Time saved (before vs. after).
   - Accuracy rate / Error reduction.
   - System uptime / Success rate of automations.
3. **Challenge Analysis (MECE):**
   - **Technical:** Bugs, infrastructure, performance bottlenecks.
   - **Process:** Workflow gaps, input data issues.
   - **People:** Training needs, user adoption status.
4. **Action Items (Next Steps):**
   - Task, Owner, Deadline, Expected Outcome.
