# 2026 Design Trends for Corporate Slides

## 1. Bento Grid Layout
Inspired by Apple's UI, divide slides into modular boxes.
- **Best for**: Combining a chart, a quote, and an image on one slide.
- **Visual Style**: Rounded corners (12px-24px), subtle shadows, and uniform padding.

## 2. Dark Mode & High Contrast
- **Background**: Deep charcoal (#121212) or rich black (#000000).
- **Accents**: Neon Lime (#CCFF00), Electric Blue (#00E5FF), Vibrant Violet (#BF5AF2).
- **Text**: White or off-white (#F5F5F5) for maximum readability.

## 3. Massive Typography
- **Headlines**: 80pt to 120pt, occupying ~50% of the screen.
- **Fonts**: Modern sans-serifs (Inter, SF Pro, Montserrat).
- **Function**: The text *is* the graphic.

## 4. Glassmorphism
- **Effect**: Semi-transparent layers with background blur (10px-20px).
- **Usage**: Use for data callouts or overlaying text on complex backgrounds.

## 5. 9:16 Aspect Ratio
- **Purpose**: Mobile-first "leave-behind" decks sent via messaging apps.
- **Design**: Vertical scroll or swipe, large touch-friendly buttons if interactive.
