# 2026 Design Trends for Corporate Presentations & Spatial Experiences

## 1. Spatial Computing & The Z-Axis (Immersive Depth)
Moving beyond flat 2D slides, design for depth to suit AR/VR environments.
- **Floating UI Elements**: Key data and headlines should appear in a "closer" layer than background imagery.
- **Parallax Backgrounds**: Layers that move at different speeds to create a sense of physical space.
- **3D Assets & Models**: Use of high-quality, real-time rendered 3D objects instead of flat PNGs.

## 2. Bento Grid & Glassmorphism 2.0
- **Bento Grids**: Modular organization for multiple data points on a single slide.
- **Refined Glassmorphism**: More subtle background blurs (15px-30px) with high-quality edge lighting (outer glow/border).
- **Soft Neomorphism**: Using subtle shadows and highlights to make UI elements look like they are molded from the background surface.

## 3. Dark Mode & Neon Accents (The "Cyber-Executive" Look)
- **Background**: Deep obsidian (#080808) or dark charcoal (#121212).
- **Primary Accents**: High-visibility colors like Cyber Lime (#CCFF00), Electric Teal (#00F5D4), and Neon Orchid (#9D00FF).
- **Function**: High contrast for screen clarity and a premium, modern aesthetic.

## 4. Massive Typography as Graphic Element
- **Headlines**: Use heavy, expressive sans-serif fonts (Inter, Montserrat, Monument Extended).
- **Size**: 80pt - 140pt range.
- **Technique**: Text often overlaps images or is clipped with textures/gradients to act as the main visual anchor.

## 5. AI-Generated & Physics-Based Motion
- **AI Custom Imagery**: Using hyper-realistic, branded AI-generated visuals that perfectly match the narrative.
- **Physics-Based Transitions**: Animations (Morph/Magic Move) that feel like they have weight and momentum (elastic easing, momentum-based sliding).

## 6. Mobile-First (9:16) "Leave-Behind" Decks
- **Purpose**: Decks designed to be consumed on smartphones via messaging apps (Vertical Scrolling).
- **Interactivity**: Large, thumb-friendly touch zones for navigating sections.
