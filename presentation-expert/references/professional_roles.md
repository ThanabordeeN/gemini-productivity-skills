# Professional Roles in 2026 Presentation Design

1. **Spatial Experience Designer**: Specializes in designing for the Z-axis, creating depth, volumetric data visualization, and immersive AR/VR environments.
2. **AI Content Strategist / Prompt Engineer**: Crafts high-quality prompts to generate branded AI imagery and leverages AI for content personalization and data refinement.
3. **Motion Designer (Physics Focus)**: Focuses on fluid, natural-feeling transitions and animations that guide the audience's eye through complex narratives.
4. **Presentation Designer**: Expert in layout (Bento/Glassmorphism), typography (Massive Type), and overall visual narrative in 2D and 3D.
5. **Pitch Deck Consultant / Business Strategist**: Focuses on persuasive content, investor psychology, and aligning the narrative with high-level corporate goals.
6. **Data Storyteller**: Specializes in turning complex datasets into clear, human-centric micro-stories and narrative headlines.
