# Professional Roles in Presentation Design

1. **Presentation Designer**: Focuses on visual narrative, layouts, and advanced tool usage (PowerPoint/Keynote/Figma).
2. **Pitch Deck Consultant**: Focuses on persuasive content, investor psychology, and business strategy.
3. **Content Strategist / Copywriter**: Crafts narrative headlines and ensures concise, powerful language.
4. **Art Director**: Ensures brand consistency (Brand DNA), premium aesthetics, and high-level visual direction.
